Services

Pediatric Services

Our pediatric team supports children of all ages with individualized therapy plans that build strength, confidence, and independence—at home, school, and play.

Pediatric physical therapy helps children improve strength, balance, coordination, and mobility through play-based, age-appropriate exercises.

Our therapists create personalized plans that support developmental milestones and help kids move with confidence in everyday activities.

Occupational therapy supports fine motor skills, sensory processing, and daily living activities so children can thrive at home and in school.

Speech therapy helps children develop communication, language, and feeding skills with engaging, family-centered care.

Aquatic therapy uses warm water to support movement, reduce joint stress, and build strength in a fun, low-impact environment.

ABA therapy focuses on building communication, social, and adaptive skills through evidence-based, individualized behavioral support.
